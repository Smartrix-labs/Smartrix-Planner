Privacy Policy for Smartrix Planner
Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Smartrix Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Smartrix Planner.

1. Information We Collect
Smartrix is a local-first application.

Local Data: All tasks, settings, and timer history are stored locally on your device.
Google User Data: If you choose to sign in with Google for Cloud Sync/Backup:
We request access to your email address and profile name to display your signed-in status.
We request access to your Google Drive (drive.file scope) to create and manage application backups.
2. How We Use Your Information
Google Drive Access: Smartrix uses the Google Drive API specifically to:
Create a "Smartrix" folder in your Google Drive.
Upload (Backup) your local task data to this folder properly securely.
Download (Restore) your backups from this folder to your device.
We DO NOT read, view, or modify any other files in your Google Drive. We only access files created by Smartrix.
No External Servers: Smartrix does not send your task data or Google user data to any external analytics servers, third-party trackers, or databases owned by the developer. Your data stays between your device and your Google Drive.
3. Data Sharing
We do not share your personal data with third parties. Your backups are private to your Google Drive account.

4. Security
Smartrix communicates directly with Google API servers using secure HTTPS connections. Your authentication tokens are stored locally on your device in secure storage (localStorage/Electron store) and are never transmitted to us.
